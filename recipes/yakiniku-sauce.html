Yakiniku Sauce

Description

Enjoy this rich, sweet, tangy dipping sauce with grilled meats or hot pot for a fantastic Japanese treat this autumn! Yakiniku sauce blends flavors of sake, mirin, soy sauce, ginger and garlic into a delicious concoction which serves as a lovely companion to all kinds of foods.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp sake
  • 2 Tbsp mirin
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 Cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 Tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 Clove garlic
  • 1 Tsp roasted sesame oil
  • 1 Tsp toasted white sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan
  2. Turn heat to medium and bring to a boil
  3. Stir until all ingredients are dissolved together
  4. Remove from heat and allow to cool
