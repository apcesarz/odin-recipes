Protein Shake

Description

Bulk up with protein and extra calories provided by this hearty banana peanut butter chocolate smoothie! A scoop of chocolate protein powder provides that little extra boost of daily protein while peanut butter and real frozen bananas adds a natural taste.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz milk
  • 33 g peanut butter
  • 33 g chocolate whey protein powder
  • 4 slices frozen banana

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
