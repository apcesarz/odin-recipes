Ants on a Log

Description

The only kind of ants mom wouldn't mind having in the kitchen! This quick snack is crunchy, sweet, and salty providing a quick pick-me-up for kids both young and young at heart.

Ingredients

  • 1 celery stalk, sliced into 2 in. lengths
  • Peanut butter
  • Raisins

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter into the trough of each celery slice
  2. Place the raisins in a line in the peanut butter
  3. Enjoy!
